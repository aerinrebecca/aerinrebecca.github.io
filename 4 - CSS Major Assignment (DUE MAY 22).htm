All About Downingtown S.T.E.M. Academy

Created May 2019

S.T.E.M. is located at 335 Manor Ave, Downingtown, PA 19335

S.T.E.M.'s phone number is (610) 269-8460

The S.T.E.M. spirit colors are blue and gold.

Stem is an IB school

There are currently 791 Students as of 2018-2019 school year.

Stem was founded in 2011.

There are students from grades 9-12.

There is a 14:1 student to teacher ratio.

The library is called the KoCo (Knowledge Commons) and the cafeteria is called the StuCo (Student Commons).

There is a 12-day cycle for the students' classes to rotate.

Stem has 1-7 days (All classes), 1-4, and 5-7 days with hour and a half classes.
