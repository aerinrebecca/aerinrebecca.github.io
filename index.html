What is FIRST?

First Robotics Competition (Grades 9 - 12) FRC Information
First Tech Challenge (Grades 6 - 8) FTC Information
First Lego League (Grades 4 - 6) FLL Information

FIRST is a Robotics organization created in 1989 by Dean Kamen and Woodie Flowers. FIRST has 5 Programs for students ages three to Senior year of high school. The smaller program is FLL Jr. for Kindergarten through fourth grade, and the main programs are FRC, FTC, and FLL.
